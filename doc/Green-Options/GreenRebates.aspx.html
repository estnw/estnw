Rebates and Tax Credit


The State of Washington offers sales and use tax exemption on energy generating technologies and equipment. Washington also has Renewable Energy Production Incentives and Energy-Efficent Appliance Rebate program.

Clark Public Utilities offers rebates on energy conservation appliances and on homes that built to Energy Star specifications. Clark PUD also offers loan programs for Residential Heat Pump, Residiential Weatherization and Solar Energy Equipment.

Oregon Department of Energy offers builder tax credit for "Energy Star" certified new home. A list of energy efficient products or technologies that are eligible for a tax credit can also be found on the www.oregon.gov website.
