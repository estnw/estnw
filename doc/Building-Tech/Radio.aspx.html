Quail Homes Building Green Radio Show

 

Each Saturday, Jon Girod, owner of Quail Homes, appears on 1550 AM talk radio station. “Green Building with Jon Girod” offers information on every aspect of building a sustainable, healthy, high-performance, environmentally green home.
